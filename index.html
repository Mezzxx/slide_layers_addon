Everything you need to manage layers

Built for designers and presenters who need precise control over their slides.

◆

Element Overview

See every element on your slide at a glance — labeled by actual type: Text, Image, Rectangle, Arrow, Ellipse, Table, and more.

↕

Reorder Layers

Move elements forward, backward, to the front, or to the back. Use buttons, drag-and-drop, or keyboard shortcuts.

●

Hide & Show

Toggle visibility on any element to reduce clutter while you work. Hidden elements are preserved and restored with one click.

⎀

Bidirectional Selection

Click a layer to select it on the canvas. Select an element on the canvas to see it highlighted in the panel. Always in sync.

☰

Multi-Select

Ctrl+click to toggle, Shift+click for range select, Ctrl+A for all. Bulk hide and show across your selection.

⌨

Keyboard Shortcuts

Alt+Arrow to reorder, H to hide/show, F2 to rename, Escape to deselect. Keep your hands on the keyboard.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Move layer up Alt↑
Move layer down Alt↓
Toggle hide / show H
Rename layer F2
Select all CtrlA
Clear selection Esc
